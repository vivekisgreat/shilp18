SHILP 2018

ANNUAL TECHNICAL FEST OF CIVIL ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT IIT (BHU)

Having a rich legacy of conducting expert event management, covering various perspectives that promote Civil Engineering culture across the masses, Shilp is one of the most constructive Civil Engineering fests of India. As precedence indicates, we've not just had a collaborative effect on the participants, but also promoted learning through practice, all of which has contributed to its rich legacy.

Intriguing guest talks are held every year to enrich the student with knowledge of some of the most raging topics of Civil engineering of the decade.

This year, we also bring an illustrious panel constituting of prominent personalities of the core engineering start-up world to discuss with the participants the key factors of being an entrepreneur. Intellect floats around like never before as the participants build their ideas to overcome challenges presented by our events. The idea we promote is simple - "Build Better".

  • Everyone is required to carry a screenshot of your payment.

  • Please bring 2 passport sized photographs.
  • Facilities provided: Lodging from 4th to 7th of october
  • Security of laptops and other belongings will be the responsibility of participants only. Shilp will not be responsible for any loss or damage to the belongings of the participants.
  • Participants are to bring along all the material required for the respective events.
  • Kindly note that the accommodation is strictly on a shared basis and any requests for individual rooms or any complaints in this regard shall not be entertained.
  • Random checks would be made to avoid any illegal stay at the campus. Any team failing to produce their receipts of accommodation would be heavily fined and disqualified.
  • Alcohol, Cigarettes, drugs, any intoxicants, sharp objects and explosives of any kind are strictly prohibited inside the campus. Any other item if deemed unsafe will be prohibited. The decision of Shilp team will be final in case of any disputes.
  • There are SBI and Bank of Baroda ATMs inside the campus. Theres also an AXIS bank ATM just outside the campus, near Hyderabad Gate.
  • All gates except the main gate (Lanka gate) will close by 10PM. So you are advised to reach your hostel before 10PM.
  • In case of disputes, final decision rests with SHILP team
